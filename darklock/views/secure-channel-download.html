Ridgeline

Private, encrypted messaging built for teams and individuals who care about security.
v2.0.0 APRIL 2026 E2E ENCRYPTED

Ridgeline keeps your conversations private by default. Messages are encrypted on your device, relayed without exposing plaintext, and never decrypted on the server. It is built on modern, audited cryptographic patterns including X3DH, Double Ratchet, and XChaCha20-Poly1305.

Multi-Layer EncryptionUses X3DH key exchange and Double Ratchet for forward secrecy.
Zero-Knowledge RelayOur relay handles delivery, not message content.
Encrypted File SharingFiles are encrypted before upload and decrypted only by recipients.
Lock Screen ControlsCustomize the lock experience with themes, motion, and color options.
Camera CaptureCapture and share photos directly from your camera.
Self-Destructing MessagesSet automatic expiry timers for sensitive messages.
Chat PersonalizationAdjust backgrounds, bubble styles, and color palettes to your taste.
Profiles & NameplatesCreate personalized profiles with flexible layout options.
Screenshot ProtectionReceive alerts when a screen capture is detected.
Clipboard Auto-ClearCopied text is cleared automatically after a short timeout.
