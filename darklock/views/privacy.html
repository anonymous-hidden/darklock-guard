Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

Darklock is built around the idea that your data belongs to you. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What We Collect

We collect only what is needed to provide the service:

  • Username and email address (used for your account)
  • Hashed password (we never store your plaintext password)
  • Account creation date and last login timestamp
  • Discord user ID, if you link your Discord account
  • Server configuration settings you choose for your Discord servers

What We Do Not Collect

  • Message content from Discord servers
  • Browsing history or tracking cookies
  • Payment information (Darklock is free)
  • Location data

How We Protect Your Data

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt before storage. Sessions are managed through secure, HTTP-only cookies with strict SameSite settings. All traffic is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Rate limiting is applied to authentication endpoints to prevent abuse.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in an SQLite database on our infrastructure. We keep automated backups to ensure your information is not lost in case of an issue. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Your Rights

You can request to view, update, or delete your account data at any time. To do so, contact us and we will process your request promptly.

Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts.

Children

Darklock is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13, in line with Discord's own age requirements.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the "last updated" date at the top of this page will change. For significant changes, we may notify users through the platform or Discord.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, reach out to us at guardianbotsystem@gmail.com or join our Discord server.
