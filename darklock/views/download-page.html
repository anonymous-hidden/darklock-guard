Darklock Guard

v2.0.0-beta.3 UPDATED FEB 21 2026

Tamper-proof file integrity protection with cryptographic verification. The Guard Service (Rust daemon) and Desktop App (Tauri + React) work together to protect your files with Ed25519-signed baselines and real-time BLAKE3 scanning.
