PRIVACY POLICY

This installer does not collect, transmit, or store personal data.

All installations occur locally using official sources (winget).

No telemetry, tracking, or analytics are used.

Your installation preferences are saved locally on your device only.

This software connects to:
  - Microsoft winget repositories (for package installation)
  - Official software vendor websites (for direct downloads)
  - No other external services

We respect your privacy and believe in transparency.
