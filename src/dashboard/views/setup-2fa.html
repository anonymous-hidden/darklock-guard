How It Works

What you'll need: A TOTP authenticator app like Google Authenticator, Authy, or Microsoft Authenticator on your mobile device.

Two-factor authentication adds an extra security step when you log in. After signing in with Discord, you'll need to enter a 6-digit code from your authenticator app.

Benefits:

  • Protects your account even if Discord is compromised
  • Prevents unauthorized dashboard access
  • Backup codes available if you lose your device
