FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade from the dashboard?

Yes. Select the server you want to upgrade, then open the payment page and complete Stripe checkout.

How do I upgrade my plan?

Log in, choose the server that should receive Pro, and continue to Stripe from this page or the payment page.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit and debit cards through Stripe. Billing and cancellation are managed from the dashboard.

Can I cancel anytime?

You can manage billing through the Stripe billing portal when a billing profile exists for the selected server.
