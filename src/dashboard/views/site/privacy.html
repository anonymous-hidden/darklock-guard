Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DarkLock ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot and related services.

By using DarkLock, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Discord

When you add DarkLock to your server, we may collect:

  • Server ID and name
  • User IDs of server members (for moderation features)
  • Message content (only for active moderation features like anti-spam)
  • Server configuration and settings
  • Audit log events for security monitoring

2.2 Dashboard Information

When you use our web dashboard, we collect:

  • Discord OAuth2 data (user ID, username, avatar)
  • Session information for authentication
  • IP address for security purposes

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Bot usage statistics and command usage
  • Error logs for debugging purposes
  • Performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our security services
  • Detect and prevent spam, raids, and other threats
  • Process moderation actions
  • Maintain audit logs as configured
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Communicate important updates about our service
  • Ensure compliance with Discord's Terms of Service

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security audits are performed
  • Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel

5. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Server Settings: Retained until the bot is removed from the server
  • Moderation Logs: Retained based on your plan (7-30 days for free, longer for premium)
  • Message Content: Processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • User Data: Deleted upon request or when no longer needed

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
  • With service providers who assist in operating our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable specific features that collect data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us on our Discord support server.

8. Children's Privacy

DarkLock is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through our Discord server or dashboard. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Discord: Join our support server
  • Dashboard: Support page
