▦ Install on Your Device

▸ iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open Safari and go to your Nova server address (e.g. http://YOUR-IP:8950)
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow at the bottom of the screen)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Name it Nova and tap Add
  5. Nova now appears on your home screen as a native-looking app!
Important: You must use Safari — Chrome/Firefox on iOS don't support Add to Home Screen for PWAs.

▸ Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Chrome and go to your Nova server address
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right
  3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
  4. Confirm the install — Nova appears in your app drawer

▸ Desktop Browser (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Open Chrome or Edge and go to your Nova server address
  2. Click the install icon (⊕) in the address bar
  3. Click Install — Nova opens as a standalone window

▸ Network Setup

  1. Make sure your Nova server is running on port 8950
  2. Find your computer's local IP: run hostname -I (Linux) or check Wi-Fi settings
  3. On your phone, connect to the same Wi-Fi network
  4. Open http://YOUR-IP:8950 in the browser
Example: If your computer's IP is 192.168.1.42, go to http://192.168.1.42:8950 on your phone.
